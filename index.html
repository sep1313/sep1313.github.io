About Us

Our Story

Sapling Energy Partners was born from a profound understanding and extensive experience in the energy savings and solar industry. Our founding members, with combined experience totaling 18 years, embarked on a journey in 2016 with a residential-focused energy savings company. Through dedication and relentless effort, our team successfully conducted over 6,000 energy assessments and facilitated the installation of more than 6 megawatts of solar, with many installations paired with advanced battery storage. However, as the landscape of the energy industry evolved and unique needs emerged, we realized the immense potential in catering specifically to small to medium-sized businesses, towns and non-profits. Thus, Sapling Energy Partners came into existence.

Who We Are

Sapling Energy Partners stands as a beacon of transparency, expertise, and genuine client service in the energy industry. Our reputation, reflected in a robust 5-star standing with our sister company, speaks volumes of our commitment to every client we serve. With an arsenal of positive testimonials from satisfied clients, we've proved time and again that our focus is on client education and empowerment. Two of our founding members, Philip and Colin, have become synonymous with the Sapling Energy brand, with accolades for their forthright consultations, deep insights into energy usage, and their knack for making even the most complex energy systems understandable.

Our Vision

We're on the cusp of a new era in energy. With record numbers of state and federal incentives and tax credits, along with over $1 billion allocated for grants, the opportunities for businesses, towns and non-profits to revolutionize their energy consumption are boundless. At Sapling Energy Partners, we envision a world where clean power is abundent, reliable and cheap. Energy doesn't just power machines; it drives innovation, output, and potential. Our mission is to help every client hit the reset button on their energy bills so they do more of what they do best.
